1930s Bungalow


This adorable Bungalow, located in Mount Desert, Maine, is just 12 minutes from Downtown Bar Harbor, and 5 minutes from historic Southwest Harbor. The house has hard wood floors, original cabinets and wood work. The house is simple and charming with lots of wood, lots of windows, nice rugs, lots of books, old prints and lovely sunsets from the living room windows.

Amenities include, washer dryer, full bathroom, parking for 2 or 3 cars, barbecue, wifi, Cable TV. Towels and linens are provided. One full bathroom, 3 Bedrooms (3 beds), sleeps up to 6, 5 in beds.

On Mount Desert Island, the house is centrally located between 5 and 15 minutes drive to all the towns and villages and several entrances to Acadia National Park. Enjoy hiking, kayaking, beaches, whale watches, historic walking tours, great shopping and dining, all nearby.

It is also about a mile and a half to one of the best swimming lakes on the island, 5 minute drive to two of Acadia's nicest mountain hikes AND the island wide free bus goes right past the drive way. You can just wave to them and they will stop for you.

135 Cottage


This cute 2nd floor apartment including a deck for gathering and observing the village life of Bar Harbor in the summer, has all the amenities needed for a super comfortable stay. There are two bedrooms with a total of 4 beds and up to 8 people can sleep in the apartment.

There is a full bathroom and a kitchen, including a microwave, coffee maker, stove and fridge. Dining for larger groups may be better accomplished out, and there are certainly many nearby restaurants to choose from, including a wonderful breakfast place right across the street.

On the quiet end of Cottage St, right in the heart of Bar Harbor, the apartment is a short stroll from the ocean, shopping, dining, supermarket, Village Green, and many historic sites, including the Criterion Movie Theater. The apartment is only a few blocks from the the beautiful town pier at Agamont park, and only a 10 minute drive to the Acadia National Park entrance. The island wide free bus available until mid September.

Amenities include, full bathroom, on street parking, wifi, Cable TV. Towels and linens are provided.

2 Bedrooms (4 beds), sleeps up to 8.
